# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in OpenDNA a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Examples of positive behavior:**
- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect for differing viewpoints
- Constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Empathy toward other community members
- Helping newcomers learn the ropes

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information
- Sexual attention or advances
- Other unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **conduct@dyber.org** (or the project maintainers via GitHub).
All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct, and to ban contributors who violate it.
